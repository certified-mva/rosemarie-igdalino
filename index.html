WHY OUTSOURCE

Why Pain Management Practices Outsource Prior Authorization

Prior authorizations in pain management are complex, time-sensitive, and highly payer-specific. Many clinics outsource this function to avoid delays, denials, and disrupted schedules. When handled inconsistently or without dedicated ownership, authorizations often result in delays, denials, and lost revenue.

For Practice Owners

  • Protect revenue by reducing avoidable denials
  • Keep provider schedules full and predictable
  • Avoid last-minute cancellations
  • Reduce staffing strain and burnout

For Office Managers & Administrators

  • Clear authorization backlogs faster
  • Reduce daily payer phone calls
  • Minimize patient frustration
  • Improve coordination between clinical and scheduling teams

Real-World Example (HIPAA-Safe)

A multi-provider pain management clinic experienced frequent procedure delays and denials due to missing documentation and missed frequency limits.

After assigning a dedicated prior authorization specialist, procedure history was reviewed before submission, LCD/NCD and payer rules were applied consistently, and authorization status was tracked daily.

Results: fewer rescheduled procedures, faster approvals, and smoother clinic operations — without increasing staff workload.
